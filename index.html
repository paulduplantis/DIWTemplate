Please note: Pathways for Mental Health is not operated by medical professionals. The information provided on this site is intended for general informational purposes only and should not be considered a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the guidance of a qualified healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding your mental health.

Pathways for Mental Health supports individuals facing mental health challenges and those who care for them, offering guidance and resources for times of need. We believe the improvement of Mental Health across all of society should be the next moonshot driven not only by institutional directives but by governments, businesses, and especially by everyday people.
